# Art & Style Guide (v0)

## Look & feel
- “Modern science game”: clean, friendly, slightly glossy, not overly childish.
- Background: soft gradients, nucleus/cytoplasm separation line, subtle particles.
- Objects: simple silhouettes with readable labels and high contrast.

## Palette
### Base colors (must match everywhere)
- A: `#2EE59D`
- T: `#FF4D6D`
- C: `#57A2FF`
- G: `#FFCC4D`
- U: `#B17BFF`

### Neutrals
- Background: deep navy (`#0B1220`)
- Panel: blue-black (`#101A33`)
- Text: near-white (`#E6ECFF`)
- Muted text: desaturated (`#A8B3D6`)

## Typography
- UI: system sans (clean, fast)
- Sequences: monospace

## Iconography / shapes
- Enzymes: rounded capsule tool shape + label badge
- Strands: ribbons/rails with base “tiles”
- Ribosome: two-lobed shell with clearly marked A/P/E sites in advanced mode

## Accessibility
- Always include letter on base tiles (A/T/C/G/U) + optional hatch patterns.
- Minimum contrast: labels readable on mobile (no thin light-gray on white).
